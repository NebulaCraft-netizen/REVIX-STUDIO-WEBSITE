Welcome to REVIX Studio

REVIX Studio is more than a development group — it’s a movement. We aim to redefine what a Roblox FPS can be. From smooth mechanics and responsive combat systems to breathtaking maps and unique community-driven features, our work is fueled by passion. This site exists to share our journey, invite new talent, and celebrate the players who inspire us daily.

Over the next few years, Roblox will see an evolution in competitive gaming. REVIX Studio plans to be at the frontlines of that transformation. With a combined experience across scripting, building, animation, and UI design, our team is committed to crafting experiences that are immersive, fun, and future-proof. Whether you’re a casual player looking for some quick matches or a competitive gamer chasing leaderboards, our games are built with YOU in mind.

Our core values: creativity, teamwork, resilience, and innovation. Every FPS we create reflects these. We want REVIX Studio to be remembered as the birthplace of iconic Roblox shooters — titles that people talk about for years.

Imagine the adrenaline rush of a perfectly timed headshot, the thrill of capturing an objective in a heated firefight, or the satisfaction of leading your squad to victory. These are the emotions we aim to deliver with every click, every shot, and every update.

This is only the beginning — our story is still being written. Stick with us as we shape Roblox history.
