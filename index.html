Welcome to REVIX Studio

The future of Roblox FPS experiences starts here.

Our Mission

REVIX Studio was founded with one goal: to push the limits of Roblox gaming. We believe Roblox is more than just a platform; it is a canvas where creativity meets code, where worlds are not only imagined but lived in. Our mission is to deliver immersive, high-quality experiences that rival mainstream gaming titles.

In 2025, Roblox is no longer a place for small projects—it has become a serious platform for professional development. At REVIX Studio, we’re blending professional development pipelines, creative storytelling, and competitive mechanics to craft an FPS that feels alive, polished, and endlessly replayable.

Why We Exist

Many Roblox games are fun but shallow. We asked ourselves: what if a Roblox FPS could capture the intensity of Call of Duty, the teamwork of Valorant, and the accessibility of Roblox? That ’s the dream we’re chasing. With powerful maps, realistic gunplay, dynamic animations, and a focus on competitive play, REVIX Studio is here to prove that Roblox can deliver AAA vibes.

Our landing page is more than a welcome mat—it’s the start of a revolution. We’re not just making a game. We’re building a community, an ecosystem, and an opportunity for gamers and developers alike.
